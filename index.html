How does AI matching work?

Our AI matching algorithm analyses data provided by survivors and uses advanced technology to identify potential matches with serial sex offenders. It takes into account various factors such as location, time and modus operandi to determine potential matches.

What support services are offered?

We offer legal help and support to survivors if a match occurs. Our team of experienced lawyers will guide survivors through the legal process, ensuring their rights are protected and they receive the assistance they need.

Is the AI matching process accurate?

While our AI matching algorithm is highly advanced and constantly improving, it is important to note that no system is perfect. We continuously work to enhance the accuracy of our algorithm and collaborate with law enforcement agencies to ensure the best possible outcomes.

How can I access the support services?

If you believe you have a potential match or need legal help, please contact our team through the provided channels. We are here to assist you  and provide the necessary support.

Can I remain anonymous?

Yes, we respect your privacy and understand the sensitivity of the situation. You can choose to remain anonymous throughout the entire process. Ouur priority is to support survivors and provide them with the help they need, regardless of their decision to disclose their identity.
